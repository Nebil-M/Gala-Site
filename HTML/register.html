The LingoBridge Global Gala: A Night of Languages and Cultures

Research has shown that language and music are good for the brain. Learning a language can stimulate the brain and can be a fun way to expand your knowledge on the world. Having a large group of people come together to exchange languages helps people learn a little bit about different languages. Additionally, it opens up discussion about linguistics so that someone can understand the concept of language just that much more. Come to the Global Gala for dances, languages, and food! Learning about cultures and language does not have to be boring, especially when the atmosphere is lighthearted and friendly.
